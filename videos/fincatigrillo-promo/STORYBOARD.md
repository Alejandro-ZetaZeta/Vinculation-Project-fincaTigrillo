## Techniques
- GSAP timelines for all animations
- Screenshot frames with border-radius + subtle shadow
- Text overlays in Syne (headlines) and DM Sans (subtitles)
- Crossfade dissolve between beats via opacity
- BGM audio loop (existing bgm.mp3)
